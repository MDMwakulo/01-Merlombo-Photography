About #1 Photographer in Kilifi

Greetings, it’s a pleasure to meet your acquaintance. I am Malombo, a professional portrait, fashion, and lifestyle photographer based in Kilifi, Kenya. While I thoroughly enjoy being behind the camera, I also relish the opportunity to step in front of it. My photographic journey began in 2019, training models on posing techniques, which has provided me with a deeper understanding of my clients’ needs. I appreciate that some individuals may find it difficult to relax and feel comfortable during a photoshoot, but I can assist with that. My ultimate objective is to ensure that the process is effortless, enjoyable, and unforgettable. It’s essential to note that our collaboration is not just about the stunning photographs we’ll create but also about the ambiance we cultivate together.
